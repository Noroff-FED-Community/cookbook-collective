Spaghetti with tomato sauce

By: Henrik

This classic spaghetti with tomato sauce recipe is a simple and delicious dinner option that can be made in just a few easy steps.

Ingredients:

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Grated Parmesan cheese, to serve

Instructions:

  1. Cook the spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a separate pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat and add the chopped onions and minced garlic. Cook until the onions are translucent.
  3. Add the canned chopped tomatoes, dried basil, dried oregano, salt, and pepper. Bring the sauce to a simmer and let it cook for about 10 minutes.
  4. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the tomato sauce. Mix well.
  5. Serve hot with grated Parmesan cheese on top.

Enjoy!
