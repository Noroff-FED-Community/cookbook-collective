Moist Lemon Cake

By: Jon Henrik Åvitsland

Fluffy, tangy, and easy to make!

Supreme moist pound cake, bursting with fresh lemon flavor, topped with easy lemon glaze.

This cake is a winner, a one-bite - instantly hooked cake!

Ingredients

10 servings

The Lemon Pound Cake

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 Tbsp lemon zest
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, room temperature
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs, room temperature
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 Tbsp lemon juice, about 1/2 lemon
  • 1/2 cup buttermilk

The Lemon Syrup:

  • 1/4 cup lemon juice, about 1 lemon
  • 3 Tbsp powdered sugar

The Lemon Icing:

  • 1 cup powdered sugar, sifted
  • 1,5 Tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 Tbsp milk

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 180°C
  2. Grease a 25x15 cm loaf pan of similar
  3. Combine flour, baking powder, salt and lemon zest in a bowl and set aside for now.
  4. Cream the butter and sugar together until pale and fluffy. Feel free to use stand mixer fitted with the paddle attachment or use an electric mixer of your choice. Scrape te sides of the bowl as needed.
  5. While mixing at low-speed, add eggs one at the time, then beat in the vanilla extract and lemon juice. Beat on medium-high speed until combined.
  6. Set the mixer to low, then add about one-third of the flour mixture and mix until almost combined, then add half the buttermilk and mix until just combined. Repeat with another third of flour mixture and then the least half of the buttermilk, at the end add the last third of flour mixture. Beat this until just incorporated.
  7. Pour the batter into the prepared loaf pan and bake for 45-55 minutes until golden brown, stick a toothpick into it and if it comes out mostly clean the cake is ready. Baking times vary from oven to oven, so keep an eye out!
  8. Cool off the cake for about 15 minutes in the pan. Now stir together the lemon syrup. After 15 minutes carefully invert the loaf pan, and move the cake over to a cooling rack or similar. Now brush the syrup over the cake while it is still hot.
  9. Now that the cake is completely cooled off, combine the icing ingredients. Start with 1 Tbsp lemon juice and milk, add the remaining juice as you see fit. The icing should be thick and not running- Now that you have mixed the icing together its time to pour it over the cake. Dry before serving!
