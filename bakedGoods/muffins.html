Blueberry Muffins

By: Henrik

These tender and moist blueberry muffins are bursting with juicy blueberries, making for a perfect breakfast or snack.

Ingredients:

  • 250g all-purpose flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 100g unsalted butter, melted
  • 180ml milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 150g granulated sugar
  • 200g blueberries

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 200Â°C and line a muffin tin with muffin cups.
  2. In a medium bowl, mix together flour, baking powder, and salt.
  3. In another bowl, mix melted butter, milk, eggs, and sugar until well combined.
  4. Add dry ingredients to the wet ingredients mixture and stir until just combined.
  5. Fold in blueberries.
  6. Spoon batter into muffin cups, filling each about two-thirds full.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes or until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean.
  8. Let cool for a few minutes in the muffin tin, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.

Enjoy your fresh Blueberry Muffins!
