Focaccia

By: Anna

Delicious, flat, oven-baked Italian bread. Ideal as side dish or sandwich bread.

Ingredients:

  • 2 l wheat flour
  • 1 l water
  • 50 g yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 tbsp olive oil

Ingredients topping:

  • 1/2 dl olive oil
  • flake salt
  • rosemary, fresh or dried

Instructions:

  1. Dissolve the yeast in lukewarm water.
  2. Add olive oil, salt and sugar.
  3. Knead in wheat flour.
  4. Add a little olive oil towards the very end so that it spreads around the dough.
  5. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and leave to rise for 45 minutes or more, until doubled in size.
  6. Pour the dough into a baking paper-lined large pan. Pat the dough until it covers the form. Let the bread rise under plastic for at least 30 minutes.
  7. Poke holes in the dough with your fingers. If necessary, put a little of the oil on your finger to prevent the dough from sticking to your fingers.
  8. Drizzle olive oil over the risen bread and sprinkle over flaked salt and rosemary.
  9. Roast on the bottom rack in a hot oven at 225 degrees (top and bottom heat) for 30-40 minutes.
  10. Cool on a rack.

    11. Enjoy!
