Delicious cinnamon buns

By: Karl

You know that fluffy, sticky and delicious center in a cinnamon bun? With this recipe you get that in every bite! Just follow the recipe below and you will soon be enjoying this mouthwatering experience.

What you need

Sweet yeast dough

  • 5 dl milk
  • 1 egg
  • 50g yeast
  • 150g sugar
  • 1kg wheat flour
  • 1tbsp cardamom
  • 1tsp salt
  • 125g butter (room temperature)

Filling

  • 150g butter (room temperature)
  • 1dl brown sugar
  • 2tbsp cinnamon

For brushing

  • 1 egg

What you do

  1. Dough: Add milk and egg to a mixing bowl and crumble in the yeast. Mix until the yeast is dissolved.

  2. Add sugar, wheat flour, cardamom, and salt. Knead for 10 minutes. If you knead the dough long enough, it will heat up enough that you don't need to heat the milk in advance.

  3. Add room temperature butter in small cubes and knead until the dough comes away from the bowl and you can stretch the dough without it tearing. This takes an additional 10-15 minutes.

  4. Cover the dough with plastic wrap or a clean kitchen towel and let it rise for about 45 minutes.

  5. Filling: Mix room temperature butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon in a separate bowl to make the filling.

  6. Cinnamon buns: Sprinkle some flour on the countertop and take the dough out of the mixing bowl. Quickly knead it with your hands. Roll out the dough into a rectangle, about 30x50 cm. Spread the filling over the entire surface.

  7. Cut the dough into slices about 1 cm thick, and place them fairly close together in a baking dish or pan.

  8. Cover the buns with plastic wrap or a clean kitchen towel, and let them rise for an additional 30 minutes.

  9. Bake the buns in the middle of the oven at 225 degrees Celsius for about 10 minutes until they are golden and fluffy.

    10. Finally, feel free to sprinkle powdered sugar on top before serving!
